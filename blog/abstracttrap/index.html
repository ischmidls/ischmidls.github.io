How logic converted me to geometry.

Constructive Logic

  • Finite semantics for fragments of intuitionistic logic, Felipe S. Albarelli and Rodolfo C. Ertola-Biraben

  • Intuitionistic Logic Model Theory and Forcing, Melvin Fitting, 1969 . . . Again, emphasis on Frege’s project rather than topological approach to forcing

  • John Bell, The development of categorical logic a historical look into categorical logic

  • THE POINT OF POINTLESS TOPOLOGY, PETER JOHNSTONE, 1983 using locales (duals of frames — complete distributive lattices) to generalize topology to topos and thus to constructive math . . . not just topology for logicians

  • Topology via Constructive Logic, Steven Vickers, 1996 . . . observe continuity through merely using reason as in programming computers

Topos and Homotopy

  • Grothendieck’s Relative Point of View (Base Schemes), Hilado

  • HoTT: Homotopy Type Theory

  • CATEGORY THEORY IN CONTEXT, Emily Riehl, 2016 made available by Riehl

    • advanced but comprehensive pure category theory

  • Categorification, John Baez, 1998

-Homotopy Cardinality via Extrapolation of Morava-Euler Characteristics, [Lior Yanovski](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Yanovski%2C+L)

  • NOTES ON TOPOSES AND LOCAL SET THEORIES, JOHN L. BELL, 1992: Revised 2020 made available by Bell. Approach Topos through logic rather than categories or topology

  • “Sets in homotopy type theory”, Egbert Rijke & Bas Spitters marries topos with homotopy types.

  • An Introduction to Category Theory: The Solutions, Harold Simmons, 18 September 2011

  • Puzzling Through Exact Sequence, Ravi Vakil, 2021

  • Intro Homotopy Types

  • Intro String Diagrams

  • Dependent Types at Work

  • Algebraic Topology Prieview, Harvard Prof. Rastern

  • Intro Topos, Baez

  • Why study algebraic geometry? Theorems, conjectures, resources

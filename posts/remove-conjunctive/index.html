Replace conjunctives

Conjunctive phrases join two independent clauses. They're like typical conjunctions (and, or, but, yet, ...). An example is "The dog sat. And, she ran." Here, "And" functions as a conjunctive phrase. They are form of transition words. This program replaces an expression falling between a newline and a comma without any extra spaces. So, this program replaces conjunctive phrases and syntactic analogues. Jouis!

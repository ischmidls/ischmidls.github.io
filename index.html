    About

    Hi, my name is Izak. I am a math student at OSU with some experience in programming & tutoring.

    I am broadly interested in Homotopy, Types, & Topoi. But, namely "'what makes the desert beautiful,′ said the little prince, ‘is that somewhere it hides a well'" ... my cryptic (perhaps romantic) way of saying, math is difficult in different ways for everyone. It is work. But, we can hope that each person holds some unique well for which the rigors of math have some beauty.

    In Stayton, Oregon, I grew up with plenty of grass fields & forests. It is beautiful. But, more importantly, I grew up with many supportive, genuine people.

    Growing up, I also enjoyed scouts, theater, science fairs, & miscelaneous activities.

    Reading & learning occupy my efforts & my imagination more than I could wish for. It is even better when I can share these things.
